About Us

Agarwal Provision Store is a premier FMCG distribution company based in Tinsukia, Assam, with a legacy of excellence in serving retailers and customers across the region. As trusted distributors, we partner with India's leading brands to deliver quality products with unmatched service.

Our extensive network and efficient supply chain ensure timely deliveries and consistent product availability. We specialize in household essentials, personal care products, and food & beverage items, catering to the diverse needs of our clients.
